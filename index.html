About the Hackathon

Climate Action Hackathon 2025 challenges you to solve city problems like heat and floods through creative, digital solutions.

  • The hackathon focuses on creating smart and practical ideas that help cities stay cool, manage floods, and build resilience against climate change.
  • Use technology and creativity to address urban heat and flood challenges
  • Creative ideas ready for testing or future development.

SDG 13- Climate Action

The solutions from this hackathon help cities stay safe from rising heat and floods. By using digital tools, data, and creative ideas, participants can design systems that cool down urban areas, improve drainage, and warn communities early. These solutions make cities stronger, smarter, and more prepared for future climate challenges.
